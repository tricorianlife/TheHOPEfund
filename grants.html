Educational grants

Investing in professional development.

2024

The 2024 HOPE Fund Educational Grant

The HOPE Fund was pleased to offer educational grants to support the professional development of nursing and allied health team members. The 2024 grants facilitated attendance at the AU ERAS+ World Congress of Prehab & Periop Medicine, fostering knowledge exchange, professional growth and networking opportunities.

The Australian ERAS+ Conference & 2024 World Congress of Prehabilitation and Perioperative Medicine was held from 3 to 5 October at the RACV City Club Melbourne.

Why these grants matter

Prehabilitation and perioperative medicine are transforming how patients prepare for and recover from major surgery. By funding attendance at leading conferences, we help our nursing and allied health teams bring the latest evidence-based practice back to the bedside, improving recovery and outcomes for every patient.
