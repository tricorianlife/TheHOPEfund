Remembering Peter

Peter Tsaklis was a man of quiet strength, deep generosity, and heartfelt connection. Even in the most difficult of times, he thought of others, and in his final wishes, he asked that donations be made to The HOPE Fund to support vital research and patient care.

Peter’s family, friends, and community remember him not only for the life he lived, but for the lives he continues to impact through this legacy of hope.

“Your generosity keeps his spirit alive and offers HOPE to countless others.”The HOPE Fund

All donations made in Peter’s memory will directly support The HOPE Fund’s mission to improve outcomes for people affected by upper gastrointestinal cancers, including pancreatic cancer.

Thank you for helping us honour Peter’s memory.
