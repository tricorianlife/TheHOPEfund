What your generosity has built.

Since 2018, The HOPE Fund has supported a range of research activities and innovative patient care initiatives within the areas of hepatobiliary and upper gastrointestinal surgery at various Melbourne hospitals.

Milestones

Achievements to date.

Patient care

Contribution to the implementation of an Enhanced Recovery Program

This program has optimised the treatment journey for surgical cancer resection patients at St Vincent’s Hospital, Melbourne, including extensive pre-operative education and consistent protocolised evidence-based care from a multidisciplinary team. Notable outcomes are fewer post-operative complications and decreased length of stay for patients.

Data & research

Contribution to an Upper Gastrointestinal and Hepatobiliary surgery database

This significant project tracks surgical outcomes across a number of Victorian hospitals and informs research undertaken by the unit.

People

Employment of a Clinical Research Assistant

Dedicated research support that keeps our clinical trials and registries moving, and our data rigorous.

People

Contribution to the employment of a Cancer Nurse Coordinator

A dedicated coordinator who guides patients and families through the complexity of a cancer diagnosis and treatment journey.
