Where this year’s gala gift goes

Surviving cancer isn’t the finish line.

Treatment ends, but the challenges don’t. Fear of recurrence, fatigue, anxiety, financial pressure and rebuilding everyday life follow many survivors long after the last appointment.

That is why A Night of HOPE 2026 raised toward a single, concrete goal: designing and testing one of the first evidence-based Survivorship Clinics for hepatobiliary, pancreatic and oesophagogastric cancer patients. On the night, our community reached it.

$100,000
Goal reached on the night Funding the Survivorship Clinic research project
  • PhysicalRebuilding strength and managing side effects after treatment.
  • EmotionalSupport for anxiety, fear of recurrence and mental wellbeing.
  • SocialReconnecting with work, relationships and everyday routines.
  • SurveillanceStructured follow-up and monitoring to catch problems early.
