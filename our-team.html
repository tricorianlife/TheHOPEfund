The people behind the hope.

Our board brings together leading surgeons, clinicians and professionals united by one goal: better outcomes for people facing upper GI and HPB cancers.

Our founding board members are Michael Hii, Adrian Fox and James Ostroburski. Since that time, fellow gastrointestinal surgeons and other medical professionals, including nurses, allied health professionals and researchers, along with senior Kooyong Group partners, have joined the board to support the work of the Fund.

While very busy with clinical practice, all of our board members maintain a strong interest in research and play active roles in surgical training and teaching through universities in Melbourne.

Deep specialist expertise

Our board includes highly trained medical experts in upper gastrointestinal and hepato-pancreatic biliary surgery, with specialisations in diseases of the oesophagus, stomach and adrenal glands, oesophageal and stomach cancer, liver transplantation, endoscopic surgery, reflux disease, hiatal hernia, and bariatric, liver and pancreatic surgery.
