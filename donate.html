Where your gift goes

Tracked to the last dollar.

Accountability was part of the founders’ vision from day one. Your donation is assigned to a specific project, and you can see the difference it makes.

1

Research that reaches patients

Your gift funds clinical trials and registries that find cancers earlier, spare patients unnecessary treatment, and push the boundaries of what’s possible in the operating theatre.

HIIT Cancer Project · MAPS Pancreatic Cyst Registry · AI in Surgery
2

Education that raises the standard

We train the next generation of upper GI surgeons through 18-month fellowships with the world’s leading units, so better skills reach more patients, faster.

Surgical fellowships · RACS training programs · OESO collaboration
3

Care that stands beside patients

From recovery and rehabilitation after major surgery to better support systems for families, your gift helps patients live longer, healthier lives.

Recovery & rehab programs · patient support · allied health grants
Why give monthly?

A monthly gift is the most powerful way to give.

Research teams can’t plan a clinical trial month to month. Steady, dependable funding lets them commit to multi-year studies, keep registries running, and train fellows without interruption.

When you give monthly, you become a Champion of HOPE, part of the community that keeps the mission moving all year round. You can pause or cancel at any time.

Giving with confidence

Clear, fair and secure.

✓

Tax-deductible

The HOPE Fund holds Deductible Gift Recipient (DGR) status with the Australian Tax Office. Donations of AUD $2.00 or more are tax-deductible, and you’ll receive a receipt for every gift.

↺

Fair refund policy

As a charity committed to transparency, we generally do not offer refunds on donations. If a donation was made in error, contact us within 7 days and we’ll consider it case-by-case, refunding approved amounts to the original payment method within 5 business days.

🔒

Secure processing

Donations are processed by PayWay, our PCI-DSS compliant payment partner, over an encrypted HTTPS connection. Your card details never pass through or are stored on this website.
